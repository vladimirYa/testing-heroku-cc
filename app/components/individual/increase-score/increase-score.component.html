What is my backup payment method?

When you don’t have enough money in your bank account to make a payment, the merchant will debit from your backup funding source. You can choose which backup payment method you prefer and set it as your "primary funding source".

If your payment can’t be completed using the payment funding source, we willl use your backup funding source.

To change the backup payment method:

1. Log in to your ChargaCard account.
2. Go to your Profile page and click on “Payment Method”
3. You can see all your different payment methods (primary and backups)
4. To add a new payment method, click on “Add Payment Method”

After adding your payment method you can decide which payment method you prefer to use as your primary payment source.

Important: It’s a good idea to make sure that you have enough money in your bank account before choosing it as your payment method. Many banks charge fees when you try to pay with your bank but don’t have enough money in your account.
